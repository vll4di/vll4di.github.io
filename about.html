About Me

I am a 26 year old aspiring cybersecurity student currently living in the UK. I am studying towards building a career in cybersecurity and have a keen interest in understanding how systems, networks, and the web work under the hood.

This website is a small personal project I decided to take on to get my HTML and CSS skills back into shape. As a kid I briefly played around with webpages without really understanding what I was doing — copying snippets here and there, changing colours, and calling it a day. This time around I wanted to actually learn how things work properly and build something from scratch, however simple it may be.

Hosting this on GitHub Pages felt like a natural fit — it is free, straightforward, and gives me a reason to get comfortable with Git at the same time. If you are reading this, thanks for stopping by.
