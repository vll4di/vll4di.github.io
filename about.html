About Me

I am a BSc student and aspiring cybersecurity professional currently living in the UK. I am also studying towards completing two certifications: Cisco CCNA and EC-Council CEH. Besides networking and cybersecurity I have a strong interest in everything linux and open source in general, currently daily driving an Arch setup installed and configured by myself.

This website is a small personal project I decided to take on to get my HTML and CSS skills back into shape. As a kid I briefly played around with webpages without really understanding what I was doing pretty much just copying snippets of html here and there, changing colours, and calling it a day. This time around I wanted to actually learn how things work and build something from scratch, however simple it may be.

Hosting this on GitHub Pages since it's free, straightforward, and gives me a reason to get comfortable with Git at the same time. If you are reading this, thanks for stopping by.
